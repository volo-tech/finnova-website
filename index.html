Why Finnova


India’s healthcare sector is growing rapidly, projected to cross USD 372 billion by 2025. Despite this scale and progress, a large section of the population still finds it hard to afford quality care. More than half of medical expenses in India continue to be paid directly by patients, often resulting in debt, delayed treatments or compromised health outcomes. Insurance, while growing is not enough and many of the treatments, especially outpatient procedures, diagnostics or elective surgeries are only partially covered or excluded altogether.

Meanwhile, traditional financing mechanisms are often not equipped to respond at the speed or sensitivity that medical situations demand. This is the gap Finnova was created to fill. By embedding financing directly into the treatment journey, we allow patients to focus on recovery while we take care of the cost. Our platform is designed for simplicity, speed, and trust enabling hospitals to offer care without payment delays and patients to receive treatment without worrying about upfront bills. At Finnova, we don’t just provide loans we provide peace of mind, financial dignity and timely access to health when it matters most.
